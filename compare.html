Which Model Should You Use?

Best for Coding

Claude 4 Opus and GPT-4o lead in code generation, debugging, and software engineering tasks. DeepSeek-Coder-V2 offers a strong open-source alternative.

Claude 4 GPT-4o

Best for Analysis

Gemini 2.0 leads in processing massive documents thanks to its 2M token context. GPT-4o and Claude 4 excel at nuanced multi-step reasoning.

Gemini 2.0 Claude 4

Best Open Source

Llama 3 (405B) and Qwen 2.5 are the most capable open-weight models. DeepSeek-V3 offers exceptional quality at low cost.

Llama 3 Qwen 2.5

Best on a Budget

DeepSeek-V3 and Llama 3.1 (8B) offer the best quality-per-dollar. GPT-4o-mini and Claude 3.5 Haiku are excellent affordable API options.
