Scaling Laws & New Paradigms

Will Chinchilla scaling continue? Inference-time compute, MoE, data quality, synthetic data.

  • Inference-time scaling: o1, Q* — search, verification, CoT at test time (compute ∝ performance)
  • Mixture of Experts: Sparse activation (1.6T params, 100B active) — better param/compute tradeoff
  • Data quality > quantity: Phi-3 (textbook data) ≈ Llama-3 8B; curation beats raw scale
  • Video/synthetic data: YouTube (1000T tokens), self-play, world models, simulators
  • New objectives: Process supervision, RL on reasoning, planning, world models
Scaling Laws Deep Dive Inference Scaling

Agentic AI & Autonomous Systems

From chatbots to autonomous agents that plan, execute, and iterate over long horizons.

  • Planning: Tree-of-Thought, MCTS, LLM-based planners, neuro-symbolic
  • Memory: Long-term (vector), episodic (past runs), working (context), semantic
  • Tool ecosystems: Composio, Arcade, browser, code, APIs, OS control (OSWorld)
  • Multi-agent: AutoGen, CrewAI, LangGraph, Swarm — specialization, debate, verification
  • Benchmarks: SWE-bench, MLE-bench, WebShop, Mind2Web, GAIA, τ-bench
Agents Guide SWE-bench

Embodied Intelligence & Robotics

Foundation models for the physical world — sim-to-real, dexterous manipulation, humanoids.

  • Foundation models: RT-1/RT-2, PaLM-E, Octo, OpenVLA, GR-1, π₀ (Physical Intelligence)
  • Approaches: Imitation learning, RL, diffusion policies, VQ-VAE tokenization, VQ-BeT
  • Hardware: ALOHA, Franka, Unitree, Figure, Tesla Optimus, 1X, Apptronik, Agility
  • Simulators: Isaac Sim, MuJoCo, PyBullet, Genesis, ManiSkill, RoboCasa, XLand
  • Key challenge: Sim-to-real gap, data scarcity, safety, long-horizon credit assignment

AI for Scientific Discovery

Automating the scientific method — hypothesis, experiment, analysis, theory building.

  • Materials: MatterGen, M3GNet, Crystal GNNs, inverse design (batteries, catalysts, PV)
  • Biology: AlphaFold 3, ESM-3, ProteinMPNN, RFdiffusion, GeneFormer, scGPT, virtual cells
  • Climate: GraphCast, FourCastNet, Pangu-Weather, NowcastNet, NVIDIA Earth-2, digital twins
  • Physics/Math: Neural operators (DeepONet, FNO), AlphaGeometry, Lean/Isabelle proof assistants
  • Chemistry: RXN, ChemBERTa, MolCLR, retrosynthesis, reaction prediction
Science Deep Dive AlphaFold

Multimodal & World Models

Models that understand and simulate the world across all modalities — the path to general intelligence.

  • Any-to-Any: ImageBind, CoDi, AnyMAL, Unified-IO, Transfusion (discrete + continuous)
  • Video as world model: Sora, Gen-3, Luma, Kling, Stable Video Diffusion, V-JEPA
  • 3D/4D: Gaussian splatting, NeRF, 4D generation, spatial intelligence
  • Audio unification: AudioPaLM, SpeechGPT, VALL-E, seamless streaming
  • Goal: Unified representation space for perception, reasoning, action, generation
Multimodal Guide Sora Paper

Alignment, Safety & Governance

Ensuring increasingly capable systems remain beneficial and controllable.

  • Scalable oversight: Debate, amplification, recursive RM, weak-to-strong generalization
  • Interpretability: SAEs (sparse autoencoders), circuit analysis, automated interpretability
  • Control: Activation steering, representation engineering, circuit breakers
  • Governance: EU AI Act, compute governance, model registration, incident reporting
  • Evals: Dangerous capabilities (CBTR), alignment evals, red-teaming standards
Alignment Overview Interpretability

Societal & Economic Transformation

Labor markets, inequality, education, geopolitics, environment, human-AI collaboration.

  • Labor: Augmentation vs automation, task-level exposure, reskilling, universal basic compute
  • Economics: $15.7T GDP impact by 2030 (PwC), compute as new oil, winner-take-most dynamics
  • Education: Personalized tutors, curriculum transformation, assessment redesign
  • Geopolitics: Chip export controls, sovereign AI, talent migration, compute diplomacy
  • Environment: Training carbon, inference at scale, water, hardware supply chain, green AI
